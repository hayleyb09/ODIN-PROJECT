Take a Ride on the Lasagna Side

Who doesn't love Lasagna?

We all know and love this popular dish. Orginating in Italy,
Lasagna leaves a long lasting legacy of cheesy tomatoe goodness

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb of Ground beef
  • 2 jars pasta of sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Seasoning and spices

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook ground beef until all the pink is gone.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, spices, and seasonings.
  4. Remove pasta from heat and drain. Then began layering.
  5. Once sauce is done, mix the ricotta, cheese, and eggs together in a bowl.
  6. Now began layering. Pasta,ricotta cheese,sauce. Repeat until pan is full.
  7. Cover and place in oven for a hour.
