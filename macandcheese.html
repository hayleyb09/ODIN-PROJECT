Mouth Watering Mac and Cheese

Try this super cheesy mac recipe!

This cheesy mac originated in the southern states and became a staple in black kitchens.
The combination of different cheeses and flavors combined make a delictable savory side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 16 oz box of Macaroni noodles
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Mustard
  • Paprika
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 4-5 different varietys of cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Half stick of butter
  • Flour
  • 1-2 eggs

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles in pot with water unil al dente
  2. Heat up half a stick of butter.
  3. Add a little bit of flour to the butter and mix until no longer lumpy.
  4. Pour in can of evaporated milk.
  5. Chop up and add your choice of cheeses to the pot
  6. Let cheese melt little by little on med-low heat and stir occasionally.
  7. Stir in heavy cream and continue to allow cheese to cook until fully melted.
  8. Add salt, pepper, paprika, and mustard
  9. Remove noodles from pot and drain. DO NOT RINSE.
  10. Add noodles to pan and add egg to noodles and mix around
  11. Pour melted cheese mixture on noodles and throw shredded cheddar on top.
  12. Place in oven for 45 mins or until top is slightly brown.
